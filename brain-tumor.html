What does WHO Grade IV Glioma mean?
N
WHO Grade IV Glioma (Glioblastoma Multiforme) is the most aggressive primary brain tumor. Key features:

• Rapid growth with central necrosis
• Mean survival: 14–16 months with treatment
• Treatment: Maximal resection + Stupp protocol (RT + temozolomide)
• MGMT methylation status is a key prognostic marker
